Description

This creamy Shrimp Alfredo Pasta is a rich and comforting dish made with tender pasta coated in a homemade Alfredo sauce of butter, garlic, cream, and Parmesan, then topped with juicy, pan-seared shrimp. Quick to prepare yet full of flavor, it's the perfect balance of indulgence and simplicity, making it an ideal choice for both weeknight dinners and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 12 oz (about 3/4 of a standard box) fettuccine or linguine
  • Shrimp: 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined (tails optional)
  • Butter: 4 tbsp (divided: 2 tbsp for shrimp, 2 tbsp for sauce)
  • Olive oil: 1 tbsp
  • Garlic: 3-4 cloves, minced
  • Heavy cream: 1 1/2 cups
  • Parmesan cheese: 1 cup freshly grated (not pre-shredded if possible)
  • Salt: to taste
  • Black pepper: to taste
  • Parsley: chopped, for garnish
  • Optional: pinch of red pepper flakes, or nutmeg for extra flavor

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Boil salted water, cook pasta until al dente, reserve 1/2 cup pasta water, drain, set aside.

  2. Cook the Shrimp:Season shrimp with salt/pepper. In skillet, heat 2 tbsp butter + 1 tbsp oil, cook shrimp 1 to 2 min per side until pink. Remove and set aside.
